About Little Help Book

White Bird Clinic has published a human services resource guide called the "Help Book" since the 1980s. It contains a comprehensive listing of resources of people seeking health and housing resources, financial assistance, shelter, food pantries and meal sites, and help to meet other basic needs. It helps people navigate the system.
